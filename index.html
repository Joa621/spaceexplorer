About Cosmic Explorer

Our Mission

The 2025 Cosmic Explorer program aims to inspire the next generation of space enthusiasts by providing an interactive, educational platform.

Through engaging games, quizzes, and beautiful visualizations, we hope to spark curiosity about our solar system and beyond.

How It Works

  1. Visit the Game page to start your cosmic journey
  2. Click on planets to learn about them and answer quiz questions
  3. Correctly answer questions to add planets to your collection
  4. Complete your collection to earn your Cosmic Explorer Certificate
  5. Share your achievements with friends and family
